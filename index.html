Crowdsourcing Your Toolbox

Everyone has problems with tools. Whether it's having too many building up dust in your garage, or too few to complete projects around the house. What if there was an app that could solve both of these problems simultaneously? Enter Tool Shed, the innovative new tool-rental app designed to help you to crowdsource your toolbox, and make a bit of extra cash off of it!

About Tool Shed

Tool Shed is the brand-new tool-sharing app designed to allow you to rent out your spare power tools to people in your area. By signing up, you can both sign up your tools to be rented by other people on our marketplace, or browse it yourself to find the tools you need, without cluttering up your workspace with things you won't need after.

Tell Your Friends

Tool Shed will only work well if a strong community of like-minded, DIY-focused individuals is built up around it. So, if you have any friends who have projects they need to complete, or have extra tools laying around, we urge you to tell them about Tool Shed! Help build the tool-sharing community in your area!
